# QUALIOPI Compliance Checklist

## 1. Information and Communication
- [ ] Ensure that the organization provides clear information about its services.
- [ ] Maintain effective communication with clients throughout the training process.

## 2. Training and Assessment
- [ ] Verify that training programs are designed to meet the needs of the target audience.
- [ ] Implement assessment methods to evaluate the effectiveness of training.

## 3. Resources and Facilities
- [ ] Ensure that adequate resources and facilities are available for training.
- [ ] Regularly review and update training materials and resources.

## 4. Trainer Competence
- [ ] Confirm that trainers possess the necessary qualifications and experience.
- [ ] Provide ongoing professional development opportunities for trainers.

## 5. Evaluation and Improvement
- [ ] Establish a process for collecting feedback from participants.
- [ ] Use feedback to continuously improve training programs and services.

## 6. Compliance with Regulations
- [ ] Ensure that all training activities comply with relevant laws and regulations.
- [ ] Maintain records of compliance and any audits conducted.

## 7. Documentation
- [ ] Keep thorough documentation of all training activities and participant progress.
- [ ] Ensure that documentation is easily accessible and organized.

## 8. Client Support
- [ ] Provide support services to clients before, during, and after training.
- [ ] Establish a system for addressing client inquiries and concerns.

## 9. Quality Assurance
- [ ] Implement a quality assurance process to monitor training effectiveness.
- [ ] Regularly review and update quality assurance procedures.

## 10. Continuous Improvement
- [ ] Foster a culture of continuous improvement within the organization.
- [ ] Set measurable goals for improvement and track progress over time.
